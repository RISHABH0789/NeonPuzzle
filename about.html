The Developer: NYX

Welcome to the grid. I am a student and developer currently exploring the intersection of logic, aesthetics, and game design. Neon Puzzle was born from a desire to experiment with vibe coding—mixing the raw power of AI with a specific visual identity.

The game utilizes a signature palette of Deep Black, Vivid Orange, and Electric Cyan to create an immersive, high-contrast environment.

The Development Journey

Building this wasn't just about drawing lines. The most grueling challenge was the Leaderboard Syncing. Managing unique user sessions and data persistence across difficulties was a masterclass in database architecture and state management.

The Next Protocol

The journey doesn't end here. My long-term dream is to develop a full-scale game inspired by the series I loved growing up. To achieve that, I am transitioning into C# development—mastering the craft of game engineering from the ground up.
