HOW TO PLAY

1. THE MISSION

Connect pairs of glowing nodes of the same color. Your goal is to secure all nodes on the grid.

2. THE PROTOCOL

Click and drag from one node to its matching pair. Lines can move horizontally or vertically.

3. NO OVERLAP

Lines cannot cross each other. If you block a path, you'll need to re-route or delete a path by clicking it.

4. SECURE THE RANK

Complete all 5 levels as fast as possible. Only full completions are eligible for the Global Leaderboard.
