Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 25, 2026

Welcome to Neon Puzzle. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your information when you play our game.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a competitive experience on our leaderboard, we collect the following limited data:

  • Username: The name you choose to represent yourself on the leaderboard.
  • Game Statistics: Your best completion times and scores per difficulty level.

2. How We Use Data

We use this information exclusively for:

  • Displaying global rankings on the leaderboard.
  • Tracking your personal best records across sessions using local storage.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase as our backend database provider to securely store leaderboard entries. No personal email or contact information is shared with third parties unless you explicitly provide it for support purposes.

4. Data Retention

Leaderboard data is stored indefinitely to maintain historical rankings. You may request the removal of your username from our leaderboard at any time by contacting us.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

Neon Puzzle uses LocalStorage to remember your game settings, current level progress, and session status. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this policy, feel free to reach out via our official support channels provided in the "About" section of the game.
